Gardening Tips for a Thriving Garden

  • Water your plants early in the morning to minimize evaporation.
  • Use compost to enrich the soil and provide nutrients for plants.
  • Place sun-loving plants in areas that receive plenty of sunlight.
  • Regularly prune plants to remove dead parts and promote new growth.
  • Mulch around plants to retain moisture and suppress weeds.
